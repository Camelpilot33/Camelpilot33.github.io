Birotor



Integral/Predicted: 0

This is a 2D simulation of a Quadrotor simplified down to two rotors. It is controlled by a PID controller. The quadrotor has two inputs, the two rotors' thrusts, and can fully measure its state. The PID controller uses the error between the target position and the current position to adjust the rotors' thrusts. The integral term is used to correct for steady-state error, and the derivative term is used to dampen oscillations.

Controls:

  • A/D: Power left/right rotor
  • S: Give equal power to both
  • W: Enable PID assistance
  • E: Enable integral prediction
  • 1–9: Adjust power
  • Arrow Keys/Mouse: Move target
  • Space: Reset simulation
